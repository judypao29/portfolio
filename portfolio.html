Mad Libs ⇒

Play Mad Libs

Mad Libs is a fun game for all ages. It is a good way to bond with new classmates and have a good time with old friends. This game has been passed down for generations and generations because of how much fun it is.

To play, you first fill out the information requested. You need to know the difference between an adjective, noun, and verb in order to create a story that makes grammatical sense. After picking your words, you get a funny story that is created by all of the words you just chose!

Come give Mad Libs a try!
